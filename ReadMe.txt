# Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis

**Goal:** Convert casual riders to annual members by understanding usage differences.

**Key Findings:**
- Casual riders take 2x longer rides than members
- 40% of casual rides occur on weekends
- Members ride consistently throughout the week

**Tools:** SQLite | Excel |

**Report:** [Analysis_Report.docx]

**Recommendations:**
1. Weekend membership promotions
2. Market membership for long leisure rides
3. Weekend Pass trial membership
